## CONTENT COMPLETENESS RULES (CRITICAL!)

For each question, verify users have enough information to answer:

### MATCHING_INFORMATION / MATCHING_FEATURES / MATCHING_ENDINGS
- `instruction_md` OR `passage_md` MUST contain FULL option list (A-H) with meanings
- ❌ FAIL: instruction_md just says "Choose A-H" without explaining what each letter means

### MATCHING_HEADING
- `options[]` MUST have roman numerals (i, ii, iii...) with actual heading text
