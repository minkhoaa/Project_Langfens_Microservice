Correction spotlight

Verb tense

Yesterday I go to the library because I need quiet place for study.

Yesterday I went to the library because I needed a quiet place to study.

The UI keeps the learner's original wording visible, then reveals the improved version directly underneath for instant comparison.

Theory

What rule should the learner remember?

When a sentence clearly refers to a finished time in the past such as yesterday, the main verb usually needs the Past Simple form. Supporting details in the same sentence should also stay consistent with that past-time frame.

Signal word: yesterday
Main shift: go -> went
Support shift: need -> needed
Natural phrase: a quiet place to study
